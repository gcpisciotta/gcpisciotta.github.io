Visualizing the Multifamily Apartment Distribution Throughout New Jersey

multifamily
analysis
map
Published

November 22, 2022

Description

A mapping of all multifamily units for buildings with over three residential units. Viewable in 2D or 3D. Height of each cell is associated with total sum of apartment units in a half-mile radius.

Controls

drag: pan

cmd + drag (mac) or ctrl + drag (win): rotate

source: kepler.gl
